About the conference

Built for the shops doing the work

The Central States Collision Conference brings together body shop owners, managers, and technicians from across the region for two days of focused, practical programming — not sales pitches.

Expect hands-on technical training, sessions led by experienced industry speakers, and a trade show floor where you can put your hands on the latest tools, equipment, and repair technology before you buy.
